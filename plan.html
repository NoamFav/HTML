Tips & Adjustments

Meal Prep & Storage

Cook multiple portions and refrigerate or freeze as noted. The chili yields 3 meals – refrigerate one for Day 3 and freeze the extra. Quark and overnight oats can be prepared the night before. Utilize containers for grab-and-go lunches.

Protein Variations

The plan averages ~206g protein/day. To reach 212g daily, slightly increase portions of lean proteins or add an extra egg white to scrambles. Low-fat quark is an easy way to add protein – 100g extra quark adds ~10g protein for only ~52 kcal.

Macro Balance

If you need more calories or carbs, increase rice/pasta portions or add a banana to a snack. If you need to cut carbs, reduce portions of rice/bread slightly and replace with more veggies. Fats can be adjusted by changing peanut butter amounts.

Budget Tips

Stick to house brands and bulk where possible. All listed products are AH Prijsfavoriet, known for low pricing without sacrificing quality. This plan wastes nothing: almost every perishable item is fully utilized by Day 7.

Enjoyment

Rotate or season foods to keep them palatable. The core ingredients are repetitive but flavor them differently – curry powder vs. Italian herbs vs. soy-ginger marinade – so each meal feels distinct. Add zero-calorie seasonings, herbs, hot sauce, mustard, etc.

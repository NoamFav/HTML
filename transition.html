& Starting My Tech Career

Why Stay Enrolled This Year?

  • I keep student status for the entire year.
  • I maintain:
    • UM gym access
    • UM login
    • Student insurance
    • Student discounts (buses, subscriptions, etc.)

What Happens Academically?

  • I do not re-enroll for next academic year → easy, clean exit.

Why I Am Not Starting Another Bachelor or Licence

  • Another 3–4 year Bachelor means:
    • delaying my career
    • more tuition costs
    • repeating a system that doesn't fit me
    • no guarantee of better job outcomes
  • The academic model isn't suited to how I learn:
    • slow pace
    • heavy theory
    • low hands-on industry focus
    • limited real-world projects
  • The tech job market doesn't require a degree when you have:
    • a strong portfolio
    • real products used by real people
    • certifications (AWS, Kubernetes)
    • active development experience
    • entrepreneurial projects like NF Software
  • My time is better invested in:
    • building Iris, Zvezda, Auto-Commit
    • earning certifications
    • freelance work
    • landing a junior dev role
    • expanding my company
  • If I ever need a degree:
    • I can do an online Bachelor later,
    • part-time, while working,
    • without pausing my career.

Conclusion:
A new Bachelor would slow me down. The practical route is faster, healthier, more aligned with my goals, and gives me a stronger position in the tech industry.

  • Not impulsive — it's strategic.

  • The Netherlands tech market hires heavily based on:
    • skills
    • portfolio
    • experience
    • certifications
  • My portfolio is already much stronger than average Bachelor graduates.
